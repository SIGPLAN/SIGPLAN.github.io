---
title: "Seven Faculty positions in CS at the University of Oxford"
deadline: "12/17/2026"
---
The Department of Computer Science at the University of Oxford is currently advertising for seven faculty positions, three of which are open field; PL applications are very welcome.

Two of the open field positions are Tutorial Fellowships, at Wadham College and Brasenose College. These involve undergraduate teaching responsibilities, in particular for small-group tutorials, and come with substantial housing allowances. The third open field position is one of five Non-Tutorial Fellowships at Kellogg College, with teaching responsibilities associated with the part-time professional Master's programme.

Closing date is noon on 17th December, with interviews in February and March. For details see:
https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/aboutus/vacancies/vacancy-faculty-hiring.html.
